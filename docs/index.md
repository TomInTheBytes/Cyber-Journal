# Welcome to Tom's Cyber Journal

This site is a personal blog focused on cybersecurity, threat intelligence, incident response, and automation. It is a space for research notes, lab write-ups, tooling examples, and project artifacts. It may help defenders and analysts reproduce experiments, learn practical techniques, and accelerate investigations.

## What you'll find here

- Projects such as automation templates and AI experiments.
- Hands-on offensive/defensive lab research and simulations.
- Cheat sheets, and training write‑ups from hands‑on CTFs and labs.
- Anything else worth sharing.
